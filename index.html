Tripology

Helping Travelers on a budget plan their next adventure with confidence.

Concept App

Tripology aims to empower people to travel with an intelligent budget giving them confidence about spending money during travel. That is something no other product has tried or claimed to do.

The Challenge

To develop an app comparable to other travel tools, be more user-friendly, and help travelers feel more confident about budgeting by building a trip itinerary and traveling on the go.

Problem Statement

Travelers are accustomed to dishonest and hidden fees and are often frustrated with the all-in-one travel planning apps and websites. These habits need to change. Travelers continue wasting time not being able to create an itinerary that works well with a budget that they have set from the beginning.

To start the ideation process for the app, our team conducted multiple interviews and framed questions to allow for conversation that provided insights into the feelings and experiences associated with Travel. We then analyzed responses to find a general pattern from these interviews. One exciting thing that we found out is that there is an interest in keeping track of trip budgets is more common than initially thought to be.  In framing open-ended questions, our participants could answer freely, giving insight into the feelings, experiences, and emotions associated with travel, independently as well as with friends and family.

What about traveling do you enjoy most? Getting away Adventures, Inspiration & New friends Experiencing different & New cultures Relaxing with friends, family and being outdoors. Seeing new amazing things, new food, and nature. Physical activities (skiing, hiking, biking, kayaking, etc.).  Seeing new countries and their history, architecture, and museums. What aspects of planning your trip are you most in need of help with? Pricing & Packing Activities & ground transportation Daily itinerary Remote and off-grid campsites. Flights, Baggage, Transportation & Rental cars Learning about areas of interest to me finding off-the-beaten-path places. Planning what to see and visit at the destination.

We continued with a survey, finding similar wants and needs. The survey was distributed among avid travelers who regularly use multiple apps to build their travel plans. We uncovered the essential ‘pain points’ when preparing an itinerary on an already decided budget. Furthermore, using all this compiled data provided the opportunity to discover the root of the problem and how to solve it.
