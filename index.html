Tripology

Helping Travelers on a budget plan their next adventure with confidence.

Concept App

Tripology aims to empower people to travel with an intelligent budget giving them confidence about spending money during travel. That is something no other product has tried or claimed to do.

Tools Useds

Figma • Miro • Google Suite • Trello • Adobe XD & Photoshop • Invision

Go somewhere
Project Brief:

Imagine you’ve just been hired as a product designer for a new travel startup. The startup plans to launch the app once the global Pandemic ends. The startup’s goal is to build a modern-day mobile app that helps people plan their next trip, post-Pandemic.

Go somewhere
The Challenge

To develop an app comparable to other travel tools, be more user-friendly, and help travelers feel more confident about budgeting by building a trip itinerary and traveling on the go.

Go somewhere

Problem Statement

Travelers are accustomed to dishonest and hidden fees and are often frustrated with the all-in-one travel planning apps and websites. These habits need to change. Travelers continue wasting time not being able to create an itinerary that works well with a budget that they have set from the beginning.
